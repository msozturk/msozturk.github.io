Happy Learners – Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 10, 2025

Thank you for playing Happy Learners! Your privacy is very important to us. This policy explains how we handle information when you use our app.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or share any personal information. The app is designed for children and works entirely offline, except for standard app store services (like updates or crash reports handled by Google Play).

  • We do not collect names, emails, or contact details.
  • We do not access your photos, location, or microphone.
  • We do not track users or show personalized ads.

If the app requests a permission (for example, to access images for an educational activity), it is used only inside the app and is never uploaded or shared.

2. Data Collected Automatically

Some basic technical data may be collected automatically by Google Play or your device’s operating system — for example anonymous crash logs, performance data, device type, and OS version. This information is managed by Google and is not personally identifiable to us.

3. Children’s Privacy

We comply with the COPPA and GDPR-K (EU rules for children’s data). The app does not knowingly collect any data from children under 13.

If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it immediately.

4. Third-Party Services

Our app does not use third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs. All gameplay, images, and educational content are self-contained within the app.

If future updates include optional features that use third-party services (e.g., cloud saving), this policy will be updated and users will be notified.

5. Security

Even though we do not collect data, we take app security seriously. Educational content is securely packaged within the app and cannot be modified or accessed externally.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Any changes will be posted on this page, and the “Last updated” date above will be revised.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at happylearners.app@gmail.com.
